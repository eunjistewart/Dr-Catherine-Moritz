About Dr. Catherine Moritz

Dr. Moritz is a Ph.D. Psychotherapist in private practice in Jupiter, Florida since 1997. Her practice serves a wide range of patients. Additionally, she is Board Certified by the American Medical Association as a Mental Health and Wellness Coach and a Life Coach.

Dr. Moritz feels sometimes people need a therapist and other times they need a coach. Her ability to serve a diverse clientele in both capacities has proved to be efficient and effective.

She is educated in avariety of areas including, but not limited to, certifications in Post-Traumatic-Stress-Disorder, EMDR, Rapid Reduction Technique and Incorporation, Addition, Hypnotherapy, Florida Sexual Offender Counselor and Theology.

Dr. Moritz trains her patients in communication and coping skills to improve relationships in every domain ofher patients' life -including inner dialogues which can improve their confidence and self. esteem.
She also works with patients with mental health diagnosis.

"I know people are not their diagnosis. I also work with individuals who are just having difficulty in life and need a trained professional to with which to work it through." -Dr. Moritz
Dr. Moritz has taught undergraduate and graduate psychology course at Palm Beach State College and Palm Beach Atlantic respectively, has mentored and supervised new psychology graduates, and has been the Director of a therapeutic afterschool care program and the Clinical Director for the Center for Brain.
